# Secondary Indicator Interpretation Guide

## Stochastic Oscillator (STOCH)

- **Default**: K=14, D=3, Slow K=3
- **Overbought**: K>80
- **Oversold**: K<20
- **Bullish Signal**: K crosses above D in oversold zone
- **Bearish Signal**: K crosses below D in overbought zone
- **Fast vs Slow**: Fast STOCH is more sensitive; Slow STOCH (STOCHF) is smoother

## CCI (Commodity Channel Index)

- **Period**: Typically 20
- **Overbought**: >100
- **Oversold**: <-100
- **Extreme**: >200 or <-200 = very strong momentum
- **Zero Cross**: Crossing above 0 = bullish shift, below 0 = bearish shift

## MFI (Money Flow Index)

- **Period**: Typically 14
- **Range**: 0-100 (like RSI but volume-weighted)
- **Overbought**: >80
- **Oversold**: <20
- **Divergence**: More reliable than RSI divergence because it includes volume

## OBV (On-Balance Volume)

- **Interpretation**: Rising OBV = accumulation; Falling OBV = distribution
- **Divergence**: Price rising with falling OBV = weak rally, likely to reverse
- **Confirmation**: OBV trend matching price trend = strong confirmation
- **No fixed scale**: Focus on trend direction, not absolute value

## AROON (Aroon Indicator)

- **Period**: Typically 25
- **Aroon Up**: Days since highest high / period * 100
- **Aroon Down**: Days since lowest low / period * 100
- **Strong Uptrend**: Aroon Up >70 and Aroon Down <30
- **Strong Downtrend**: Aroon Down >70 and Aroon Up <30
- **Consolidation**: Both near 50
- **Aroon Oscillator**: Aroon Up - Aroon Down (positive = bullish, negative = bearish)

## SAR (Parabolic SAR)

- **Interpretation**: Dots below price = uptrend; dots above = downtrend
- **Signal**: When dots flip position = potential trend reversal
- **Best Used In**: Trending markets; gives many false signals in ranging markets
- **Acceleration Factor**: Default starts at 0.02, max 0.20

## Williams %R (WILLR)

- **Period**: Typically 14
- **Range**: -100 to 0
- **Overbought**: >-20 (note: inverted scale)
- **Oversold**: <-80
- **Similar To**: Fast Stochastic, but inverted scale
